🔄 FLIPPING & RESELLING ➤

👟 Sneaker Flipping

  • Find cheap Jordans/Yeezys at thrift stores, Facebook Marketplace, or OfferUp
  • Clean them up (use sneaker cleaning kits)
  • Resell on StockX, GOAT, or eBay for 2-5x profit
  • Pro Tip: Target limited editions and retro releases

🔥 Resources:

• StockX - Check market prices

• r/Flipping - Flipping community

📱 Electronics Flipping

  • Look for broken iPhones on Craigslist (people sell them cheap thinking they're worthless)
  • Fix simple issues (battery replacement, screen repair - YouTube tutorials)
  • Resell on Swappa or eBay for 50-100% profit
  • Pro Tip: Target iPhone 11-13 models - highest demand

📚 Learning:

• iFixit - Repair guides

• JerryRigEverything - Repair videos

🚗 Car Flipping (Advanced)

  • Find cars under $2k with minor issues (check Craigslist "by owner" section)
  • Fix cosmetic issues (deep clean, minor repairs)
  • Resell for $1k-$3k profit (check your local laws)
  • Pro Tip: Target Honda Civics and Toyota Camrys - easiest to sell
